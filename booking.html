Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a consultation?

Simply select your preferred service, fill out the form with your details, choose a date and time, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation within 24 hours.

What happens after I book?

You'll receive a confirmation email and WhatsApp message with the meeting link (Zoom or Google Meet). You'll also get a calendar invite.

Can I reschedule my session?

Yes! You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your session at no extra cost. Just reply to your confirmation email or message me on WhatsApp.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are securely processed via Stripe.
