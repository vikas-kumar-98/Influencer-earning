My Story

I started my content creation journey in 2018 with nothing but a smartphone and a dream. I was working a 9-to-5 job that I hated, and I knew there had to be more to life. So I started posting videos on YouTube — my first video got 12 views (and 11 of them were from me).

But I didn't give up. I studied the algorithms, tested different content formats, and within 18 months, I grew my audience to 1 million followers across platforms. I quit my job and never looked back.

Today, I've collaborated with 850+ brands including Adobe, Samsung, Spotify, and Nike. My mission is to demystify the creator economy and help you turn your passion into profit.

I believe that anyone can become a creator — it's not about having the best equipment or being born with talent. It's about consistency, strategy, and understanding what your audience truly wants.
